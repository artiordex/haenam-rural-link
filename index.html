About the Project

Haenam Rural Link is an experimental Smart Village project designed to prototype region-based digital linkage models. This one-day pilot aims to connect various aspects of rural life in Haenam, bringing together:

  • Living: Connecting residents with essential services and community resources
  • Startups: Supporting local entrepreneurship and innovation
  • Local Resources: Leveraging regional assets and capabilities
